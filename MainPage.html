A web platform for foreign tourists in Gyeongju

Access easily via QR code—no app installation needed! Get info on attractions, food, transport, and emergencies all in one place.

Key Features

  • Multilingual tourism info (Chinese, English)
    • Automatic translation of key location details
    • Language toggle for seamless user experience
  • Tourist attractions, restaurants, cafes with navigation
    • Recommended routes between nearby points of interest
    • Enter an address to receive nearby recommendations for attractions, restaurants, and cafes
  • Emergency response info support
    • Contact numbers for hospitals and tourist help centers
    • Essential phrases and guidance for urgent situations
